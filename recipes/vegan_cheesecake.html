Guinness Vegan Cheesecake

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • How to Make
  • Toppings

Description

A decadent chocolate cheese cake with a slightly acidic aftertaste that only a Guinness stout provide. True to vegan grade dessert, the recipe provides substitutions ensuring identical flavor and texture.

Ingredients Overview

  • Cookie Crumbs, 5.65oz
    • Bourbon cookies, Oreos or graham crackers can be used for the pie crust.
  • Vegan Butter, 3 1/2tbsp
    • Binds the pie crust. Coconut butter can be substituted.
  • Guinness Stout, 17.64oz
    • Signature deep stout flavor.
  • Dark Chocolate, 10.58oz
    • 75% or 80% dark chocolate will do. Both in cheesecake and topping.
  • Silken Tofu, 12.35oz
    • Gives dessert the cheesecake texture.
  • Vegan Cream Cheese, 5.29oz
    • Adds more creamy to the desert.
  • Cocoa Butter, 1/4 cup
    • Gives this desert a rich chocolate flavor - decadent.
  • Lemon Juice, 1 tsp
    • Adds the dairy flavor to the cheesecake.
  • Maple Syrup
    • Sweetens filling. Can be substitued with powdered baking sugar or agave.
  • Coconut Cream, same amount as chocolate measured for topping
    • Makes ganache for topping.
  • salt, 1/4 tps
    • For pie crust.

Preparation

Reduce Guinness into Reduction

On medium-to-low heat, using a saucepan, simmer for approximately 20-30 minutes. All the alcohol should evaporate leaving a reduction that is 40% of the original volume. Cool to room temperature before use.

Bake-free Pie Crust Preparation

Using a food processor, combine cookie crumbs and salt; grind into a fine crumb consistency. Then add vegan butter and process until the ingredients become dough-like. Using a 7 inch cake tin with a removable base apply parchment paper, possibly using some vegetable oil to help it stick. Take pie crust dough and evenly apply to the pan, concluding by smoothing with a spatula.

Filling

Prepare a heated water bath using a saucepan; heat until a gentle simmer. Using a heat resistant mixing bowl, preferrably pyrex or stainless steel, place on top of the saucepan avoiding contact with the water to create a double boiler. Note: container touching water is a Bain-Marie boiler with different heating characteristics. The rising steam should gently heat the dark chocolate.

With a mixer combine melted dark chocolate, Guinness reduction, silken tofu, vegan cream cheese, cocoa powder, and lemon juice on high. Add sweetener to taste. When done, evenly distribute throughout the cake tin containing the pie crust.

Chill overnight at minimum of 3 to 4 hours.

Optional Toppings

Chocolate Ganache

Using simmering hot water bath in a large pot, make a Bain-Marie boiler using a smaller mixing bowl placed directly on top of the water. Measure 1:1 ratio of any type of chocolate and coconut cream. Heat the chocolate until simmering begins at the edges, then remove and add the cream. Mix together and chill for later use as topping.
