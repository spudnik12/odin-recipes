Guinness Vegan Cheesecake

Description

A decadent chocolate cheese cake with a slightly acidic aftertaste that only a Guinness stout provide. True to vegan grade dessert, the recipe provides substitutions ensuring identical flavor and texture.

Ingredients Overview

  • Cookie Crumbs, 5.65oz
    • Bourbon cookies, Oreos or graham crackers can be used for the pie crust.
  • Vegan Butter, 3 1/2tbsp
    • Binds the pie crust. Coconut butter can be substituted.
  • Guinness Stout, 17.64oz
    • Signature deep stout flavor.
  • Dark Chocolate, 10.58oz
    • 75% or 80% dark chocolate will do. Both in cheesecake and topping.
  • Silken Tofu, 12.35oz
    • Gives dessert the cheesecake texture.
  • Vegan Cream Cheese, 5.29oz
    • Adds more creamy to the desert.
  • Cocoa Butter, 1/4 cup
    • Gives this desert a rich chocolate flavor - decadent.
  • Lemon Juice, 1 tsp
    • Adds the dairy flavor to the cheesecake.
  • Maple Syrup
    • Sweetens filling. Can be substitued with powdered baking sugar or agave.
  • Coconut Cream, same amount as chocolate measured for topping
    • Makes ganache for topping.
  • salt, 1/4 tps
    • For pie crust.

Preparation

  1. Reduce Guinness into Reduction

    • On medium-to-low heat, using a saucepan, simmer for approximately 20-30 minutes; alcohol content should evaporate leaving a reduction that is 40% of the original volume.
    • Cool to room temperature before use.

  2. Prepare Pie Cruse without Baking

    • Using a food processor, combine cookie crumbs and salt; grind into fine crumb consistency.
    • Add vegan butter and process until ingredients become dough-like.
    • With a 7-inch cake tin with a removable base, apply parchment paper. Use vegetable oil to help it stick.
    • Take pie crust dough and evenly apply to the base of the cake tin base, concluding by smoothing with a spatula.

  3. Make Filling

    • Prepare a heated water bath using a saucepan; until until a gentle simmer.
    • Using a heat resistant mixing bowl, preferrably pyrex or stainless steel, place on top of the saucepan avoiding contact with the water to create a double boiler. 1
    • Using a mixer combine the melted dark chocolate, Guinness reduction, silken tofu, vegan cream cheese, cocoa powder, and lemon juice on high. Add sweetener to taste.
    • When done, evenly distribute throughout the cake tin containing the pie crust.
    • Chill overnight at a minimum of 3 to 4 hours.

  4. Chocolate Ganache topping

    • Using simmering hot water bath in a large pot, make a Bain-Marie boiler using a smaller mixing bowl placed directly on top of the water.
    • Measure 1:1 ratio of any type of chocolate and coconut cream.
    • Heat the chocolate until the simmering begins at the edges, then remove from the heat and add the cream. Mix together.
    • Chill later to use as topping.

Credit to the original recipe of Christina of "Addicted to Dates".

1.Water touching container creates a more aggressive heating profile which is called a Bain-Marie boiler compared to the gentler heat from steam of a double boiler.
