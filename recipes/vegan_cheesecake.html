Description

Ingredients Overview

Cookie Crumbs
Bourbon cookies, Oreos or graham crackers can be used for the pie crust.
Vegan Butter
Binds the pie crust. Coconut butter can be substituted.
Guinness Stout
Signature deep stout flavor.
Dark Chocolate
75% or 80% dark chocolate will do.
Silken Tofu
Gives dessert the cheesecake texture.
Vegan Cream Cheese
Adds more creamy to the desert.
Cocoa Butter
Gives this desert a rich chocolate flavor - decadent.
Lemon Juice
Adds the dairy flavor to the cheesecake.

Cooking Steps

Reduce Guinness into Reduction

On medium-to-low heat, using a saucepan, simmer for approximately 20-30 minutes. All the alcohol should evaporate leaving a reduction that is 40% of the original volume. Cool to room temperature before use.

Pie Crust Preparation

Using a food processor, combine cookie crumbs and salt; grind into a fine crumb consistency. Then add vegan butter and process until the ingredients become dough-like. Using a 7 inch cake tin with a removable base apply parchment paper, possibly using some vegetable oil to help it stick. Take pie crust dough and evenly apply to the pan, concluding by smoothing with a spatula.

Filling

Prepare a heated water bath using a saucepan; heat until a gentle simmer. Using a heat resistant mixing bowl, preferrably pyrex or stainless steel, place on top of the saucepan avoiding contact with the water to create a double boiler. Note: container touching water is a Bain-Marie boiler with different heating characteristics. The rising steam will gently heat the dark chocolate.
