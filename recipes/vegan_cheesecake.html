Description

Ingredients Overview

Cookie Crumbs
Bourbon cookies, Oreos or graham crackers can be used for the pie crust.
Vegan Butter
Binds the pie crust. Coconut butter can be substituted.
Guinness Stout
Signature deep stout flavor.
Dark Chocolate
75% or 80% dark chocolate will do.
Silken Tofu
Gives dessert the cheesecake texture.
Vegan Cream Cheese
Adds more creamy to the desert.
Cocoa Butter
Gives this desert a rich chocolate flavor - decadent.
Lemon Juice
Adds the dairy flavor to the cheesecake.

Cooking Steps

Reduce Guinness into Concentrate

On medium-to-low heat, using a saucepan, simmer for approximately 20-30 minutes. All the alcohol should evaporate leaving a concentrate that is 40% of the original volume. Cool to room temperature before use.
