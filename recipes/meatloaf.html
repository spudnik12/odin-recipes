Description

Slightly sweet, savory, and spicy meatloaf.

Ingredients

  • Onion, 1 cup
  • Celery, 1 rib chopped fine
  • Garlic, 1 tbsp minced
  • Carrot, 1 whole chopped fine
  • Scallions, 1/2 cup chopped fine
  • Unsalted Butter, 2 tbsp
  • Salt, 1 1/2 tsp
  • Ground Pepper, 1 1/2 tsp
  • Worcestershire Sauce, 2 tsp
  • Ketchup, 2/3 cup
  • Ground Beef, 1 1/2 lbs
  • Italian Sausage, 3/4 lbs
  • Panko Bread Crumbs, 1 cup
  • Eggs, 2 large
  • Fresh Parsley Leaves, 1/3 cup minced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the onion, celery carrot, garlic, and scallions in butter, stirring occassionally for about 5 minutes.
  3. Cover the skillet and continue stirring periodically for about 5 minutes, whichthe carrots should be tender.
  4. Stir in salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce, and 1/3 a cup of ketchup. Cook 1 more minute.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the meats, eggs, vegetables, bread crumbs, and parsley.
  6. Form into a loaf and put into a rectangular baking pan with 2-inch high sides. Cover the loaf with remaining ketchup.
  7. Bake the meatloaf in the oven for 1 hour.
